Social Media: Is It Good or Bad?

Written by: Kinza Shamraiz
Date: 31 January 2026

Social media has become a major part of modern life. Platforms like Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, and YouTube are widely used by students and young people. Social media is not completely good or bad; its effect depends on how it is used.

When social media is used in a limited and positive way, it helps in learning, communication, and motivation. Students can access study material, educational videos, and exam guidance easily.

However, excessive use of social media can cause serious problems. It wastes time, increases stress, disturbs sleep, and reduces concentration. Many students fail in competitive exams because they spend too much time scrolling instead of studying.

Therefore, social media should be used wisely. Proper time management and focusing on educational content can turn social media into a helpful tool rather than a distraction.
