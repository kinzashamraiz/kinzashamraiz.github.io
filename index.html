Social Media Impact on Health

Social media platforms like Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, and Twitter are integral to modern life. They allow instant communication, entertainment, and access to vast information. Excessive use can negatively impact mental, emotional, and physical health.

Mental Health Effects: Frequent social media use can trigger anxiety, depression, and stress due to constant comparison and cyberbullying.
Sleep Disturbances: Late-night scrolling disrupts sleep cycles, causing fatigue and lower academic performance.
Physical Health Implications: Prolonged screen time causes eye strain, headaches, and sedentary lifestyle risks.
Emotional & Social Impacts: Exposure to curated content may create FOMO, loneliness, and reduce satisfaction with real-life experiences.
Conclusion: Social media is a double-edged sword. Responsible usage, balanced screen time, offline activities, and awareness are key to maintaining health.
