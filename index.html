The Silent Storm: Social Media Impact on Health

Written By Kinza Shamraiz
Student at COMSATS University Islamabad, Abbottabad Campus
Date: 1 Feb 2026

Introduction

Social media has become an inseparable part of modern life. Platforms like Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, and YouTube connect us with the world, but their excessive use can silently harm our mental health. Constant scrolling, comparison, and exposure to unrealistic lifestyles often lead to stress, anxiety, and depression.

Negative Impacts

Research shows that spending long hours on social media reduces self-esteem and increases loneliness. Notifications and likes create a cycle of dependency, where individuals seek validation online instead of building real-life connections.

Positive Uses

On the other hand, social media can be used positively for education, awareness, and staying informed. It helps students access knowledge, share ideas, and build communities. The key lies in balance and mindful usage.
